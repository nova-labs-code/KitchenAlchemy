Course Overview

Welcome to Kitchen Alchemy! Over the next six months, you'll train your palate, master heat control, develop texture and flavor, and learn professional plating and dish design. The course is structured into 26 weeks, with 7 daily lessons per week. Each day is designed to teach you real, actionable skills so you can think, taste, and cook like a professional chef.
